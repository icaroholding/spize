# ADR-0010: Self-funded Year 1; optional NLnet grant application

## Status

Accepted 2026-04-21.

## Context

The founder has 12+ months of personal runway confirmed. Taking VC at this
stage would compress the timeline to metrics the protocol isn't yet ready
to produce and would pull scope toward "fastest path to revenue" over
"protocol durability". NLnet grants (€10–50k, ~60 % success rate for
open-source infra proposals that fit their brief) are compatible with
self-funding — non-dilutive, single-day writing effort, non-blocking.

## Decision

AEX is self-funded for Year 1. We will apply to NLnet's NGI0 Core fund
when there's a cleanly writable proposal window (likely after the
v1.3.0-beta.1 + hosted launch). The application is optional — if it
grants, it buys additional runway; if it doesn't, nothing changes.

## Consequences

- No pitch-deck cycle, no investor reporting cadence, no dilution.
- Revenue pressure is real (ADR-0007 paid tier Sep 2026) but sized to
  founder runway, not board-imposed burn.
- NLnet application work is explicitly deprioritized — if a week's worth
  of engineering matters more, skip the grant.
- Year 2 fundraising decision is deferred until signal justifies it.
